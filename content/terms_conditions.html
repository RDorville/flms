Terms & Conditions for Booking and Usage of facilities at Fablab Singapore Polytechnic

The following Terms & Conditons shall apply to all persons and organizations wishing to use the facilities and/or equipment at Fablab SP (Fablab Singapore Polytechnic).

  1. Safety
    Safety is our utmost priority.
    All persons using the Fablabs must pass the Fablab Safety Course and adhere to the principles and practices of Fablab SP. We reserve the right to refuse admission, terminate or cancel any activity deemed unsafe and not adhering to the Safety principles.
  2. Users
    Fablab SP membership is open to all current full-time students and staff of the Singapore Polytechnic. All other persons (Part-time students, Public, etc) will require registration through PACE.
    Users of Fablab equipment must demonstrate the necessary skill and knowledge before being allowed to use the equipment. Training courses are available and conducted regularly for all users.
  3. Booking Process
    All bookings shall be made through the FLMS (Fablab Management System). Booking of equipment require the necessary skill certification before processing.
    Booking of equipment is charged using Fablab Credits. External users will be charged at a similar rate of SGD$1 per Fablab Credit payable using eNets at the point of booking.
  4. Cancellation
    1. Cancellations can be made and accepted up to 30 minutes before the time of use of the equipment. Upon cancellation, the credits charged will be refunded to your account.
      For External Users, there will not be any Cash/Credit refund. You will be refunded in Fablab Credits which may be applied to your next booking.
    2. FablabSP reserves the right to cancel any booking at any time without giving any reasons whatsoever. You will be refunded with the booking credit amount.
      FablabSP resevers the right to change your booking to another equivalent equipment with giving any reasons whatsover. You, however, have the right to cancel the booking without penalty.
    3. Once cancelled, any attempt my the user to reinstate the booking shall be treated as a fresh application.
    4. All cancellations must be made using the FLMS or by applying in-person to the Fablab Staff.
