General Terms & Conditions for use of FLMS at Fablab Singapore Polytechnic

The following Terms & Conditons shall apply to all persons and organizations wishing to use the FLMS application at Fablab SP (Fablab Singapore Polytechnic).

  1. FLMS
    The Fablab Management System (FLMS) manages the booking of courses, equipment, knowledgebase and materials for the Fablab SP.
    All persons who intend to use the equipment or attend courses at FablabSP must book using the FLMS.
  2. Users
    Fablab SP membership is open to all current full-time students and staff of the Singapore Polytechnic (called internal members). All other persons (Part-time students, Public, etc) will require registration through PACE.
    Booking is done using the SP ichat account
    Upon Registration you need to correctly provide the following information:
    • Your phone contact number (for emergency notifications)
    • Your personal email (for booking notifications)
    • Your status (Student, Staff or External user)
    Failure to do so will result in erroneous bookings and notifications of the transactions with Fablab SP.
    Please enter the organization as "Singapore Polytechnic".
    You may have already attended an internal course(s) on safety through your School or Department. In such cases, you will already be pre-registered with the necessary Safety skill. Kindly check your profile when you first sign-in on FLMS.
  3. Safety
    Safety is our utmost priority.
    All persons using the Fablabs must pass the Fablab Safety Course and adhere to the principles and practices of Fablab SP. We reserve the right to refuse admission, terminate or cancel any activity deemed unsafe and not adhering to the Safety principles.
    Please immediately register for a Safety course or speak to any Fablab Staff regarding Safety courses.
  4. Credit System
    The booking and use of Fablab SP equipment is based on a "credits" payment system. Although the use of Fablab SP equipment is "free" for all internal members, you will need sufficient credits to book. These credits are earned by attending courses to attain skills or by submitting knowledgebase articles.
    Kindly contact the Fablab Staff should you run out of credits and need to use the equipment urgently.
